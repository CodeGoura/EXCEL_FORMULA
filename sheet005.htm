MIN and MAX functions
Use the MIN function to get the minumum number in a range of cells. Fruit Amount Meat Amount
Use the MAX function to get the maximum number in a range of cells. Apples 50 Beef 50
Select cell D7, then use the AutoSum Wizard to add a MIN function.
 Oranges 20 Chicken 30
Now select cell G7, and enter a MAX function by typing =MAX(D3:D6).
 Bananas 60 Pork 10
In cell D15, you can use either the AutoSum Wizard, or type to enter a MIN or MAX function.
Lemons 40 Fish 50

GOOD TO KNOW
You can use either MIN or MAX with multiple ranges, or values to show the greater or lesser of those values, like =MIN(A1:A10,B1:B10), or =MAX(A1:A10,B1), where B1 contains a threshold value, like 10, in which case the formula would never return a result less than 10.
 Donuts 100 Donuts 100
Cookies 40 Cookies 40
Cakes 50 Cakes 50
Pies 20 Pies 20
